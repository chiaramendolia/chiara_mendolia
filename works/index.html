My first steps into graphic design

Downloading...

During my 3 years in eracom, art and communication school in Lausanne, and above, I learnt a lot about Swiss graphic design. I also had the chance to get directly inspireed and impacted by Japanese design style. You can see down below some graphic composition I’ve done. I learnt to structure and organize the space following the Swiss graphic grid but I also like to use my illustrations to break it down.
Branding work interest me a lot. I would say it’s very important to me to design visuals that are meaningful and consistent with a project or a brand’s direction. I also work a lot on illustrations work, which is something that I especially like to include in my designs.

Branding. Visual communication. Illustration. Graphic composition.
