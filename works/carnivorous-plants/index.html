A bunch of carnivorous plants, please

2021

Pssst.. it’s the perfect gift for your ennemies

A3 Micron Pen illustration based on an biological study of different species of the big family of carnivorous plants. I choose to focus on Sarracenia Purpurea, Nepenthes Rajah and Dionaea Muscipula to create an balanced and homogeneous composition. Their hollowed, toothed and patterned structures inspired me to compose an image based on a science species presentation structure.
