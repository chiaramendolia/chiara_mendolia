Work

I’d like a bunch of carnivorous plants, please .

2021

Micron Pen illustration based on an biological study of different species of the big family of carnivorous plants. I choose to focus on Sarracenia Purpurea, Nepenthes Rajah and Dionaea Muscipula to create an balanced and homogeneous composition. Their hollowed,toothed and patterned structures inspired me to compose an image based on jungle structure.
