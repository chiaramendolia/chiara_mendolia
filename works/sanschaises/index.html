Work

Wabi Sabi

2024

Take a seat.

Book design work that explores the Japanese mindset that embraces beauty in imperfection, impermanence, and incompleteness. Neither beginning or end, without question or answer, tradition and modernity, between materiality and spirituality, this book is an intimate and singular visual exploration.

A natural dialogue unfolds between my analog photographs taken during my six-month experience in Japan and the words of Leonard Koren.
